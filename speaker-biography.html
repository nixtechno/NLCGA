Senator Heineken Lokpobiri, Ph.D

Minister of State for Petroleum Resources (Oil), Federal Republic of Nigeria.

Federal Ministry Keynote Speaker Nigeria

Profile

Senator Heineken Lokpobiri, Ph.D, serves as Nigeria's Minister of State for Petroleum Resources (Oil). He is a senior public servant and policy leader with experience spanning governance, legislation, and national energy strategy.

His contributions to public administration and petroleum policy have made him a central voice in conversations around sector reform, investment confidence, resource development, and the broader role of energy in Nigeria's economic growth.

At the NLCGA Conference & Exhibition, his presence reinforces the event's role as a serious platform for high-level dialogue between government, investors, regulators, operators, and gas-sector stakeholders.
